Gilutongan Island

Gilutongan Island, often referred to as Hilutungan Island, is one of the treasured gems off the coast of Cebu, Philippines. It's part of the Olango Island Group, which lies between the Bohol Strait and the Camotes Sea. Known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine sanctuary, it's a hotspot for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts who wish to experience the rich underwater biodiversity of the region.

Caohagan Island

Caohagan Island is a tiny island about 7.5 kilometers off southern Olango. Among all the islands of the Olango Island Group, Caohagan has the flattest area. Its highest elevation stands at only ten meters tall and spans an area of about 50,000 square meters.

Nalusuan Island

Nalusuan is a natural island resort in the Cebu Strait, the Philippines, and one of the islands comprising the Olango Island Group. The island, which measures close to a hectare, is situated between Mactan Island and the Province of Bohol.
